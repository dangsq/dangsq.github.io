你好，我是党圣奇。一个典型的INFP，我时常幻想有一天醒来，能够以一种温柔的方式逃离这个世界的喧嚣，去到一个与世无争的地方。我有时会感到拘谨，却也因此格外珍视那些深度的思考与真诚的交流。

我喜欢画画（过去画），喜欢做手工（现在），想去学贝斯（未来吧）。运动方面，我偏爱跑步、游泳，以及一切可以独自完成的项目——在律动与静谧中，我能找到属于自己的节奏。我最喜欢的乐队是Doudou，我很喜欢她们音乐里面传达的情感。

我深深渴望世界和平，梦想每个人都能自由自在、无忧无虑地生活。与此同时，我也渴望更深入地探寻自己的内心世界，认识那个藏在思绪褶皱里的、真实的自己。


Hello, I'm Dang Shengqi. A typical INFP, I often fantasize about waking up one day and being able to escape the noise of the world in a gentle way, to a place where there is no one to disturb. At times, I feel reserved, yet this also makes me especially cherish deep thinking and sincere conversations.

I enjoy painting (in the past) and doing handicrafts (now), and I want to learn the bass guitar (in the future). When it comes to sports, I prefer running, swimming, and any activity that can be done alone—in the rhythm and tranquility, I find my own pace. My favorite band is Doudou, and I really love the emotions conveyed in their music.

I deeply desire world peace and dream of a life where everyone can live freely and without worries. At the same time, I also yearn to explore my inner world more deeply, to know the true self hidden in the depths of my thoughts.
